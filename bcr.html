☜ ☟ Bandcamp Revolution ☞ ☝

Play a game of DDR on Bandcamp with your favorite songs.
1. Get Chrome.
2. Drag one of the following links into your bookmarks bar:

Play Bandcamp Revolution! Play Bandcamp Revolution! (Hard Mode)

3. Go to a Bandcamp album or song page.
4. Click on the "Play Bandcamp Revolution" link in your bookmarks bar.
5. Click play on the song.
